Welcome To UHS

University of Abuja Health Services

It is a pleasure welcoming you to the University of Abuja Health Service (UHS). The UHS will continue to provide health care services to the University community and others. The services will continue to extend beyond the basic health care and referral for specialist consult. The services have been highly subsidized for all the clients across divides.

About Us

Tackle The Challenge Of Delivering Health Care

We deliver high quality evidence based comprehensive primary care in divisions of medical practice. We provide health promotional and disease prevention services the University community as well as to the general public. We also ensure adequate referral support. We deliver health care services in keeping with “Learning, Research and Community service”. All these are contributing to a better distribution of health outcomes, enhanced well being and quality of life of the University community.
